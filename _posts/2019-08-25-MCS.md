**Input:** binary digits (0, 1)
**Output:** 复数调制符号

| Modulation scheme | Modulation order | Downlink | Uplink \(Transform precoding disabled\) | Uplink \(Transform precoding enabled\) |
|-------------------|------------------|----------|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| π/2\-BPSK         | 1                | 0        | 0                                       | 1                                      |
| BPSK              | 1                | 0        | 0                                       | 0                                      |
| QPSK              | 2                | 1        | 1                                       | 1                                      |
| 16QAM             | 4                | 1        | 1                                       | 1                                      |
| 64QAM             | 6                | 1        | 1                                       | 1                                      |
| 256QAM            | 8                | 1        | 1                                       | 1                                      |
